About This Project

Empowering transparency in Philadelphia city government salaries

Our Mission

The public doesn't have an easy way to compare how much different city agencies pay for similar roles. This lack of transparency makes it difficult for employees to understand their worth, for policymakers to identify disparities, and for citizens to hold their government accountable.

Our goal is to build a web application that allows users to compare salaries across agencies, providing insights into pay disparities and highlighting the distribution of earnings for different roles. By making this data accessible and visual, we empower all stakeholders to make informed decisions.
